## What is Prism?
Prism is a macOS application created for professionals who work with **[scientific graphing](#)** and **[statistical analysis](#)** on a regular basis. It combines data organization, analysis tools, and visualization features into a single, consistent workflow.

The software supports a wide range of common statistical methods, including hypothesis testing, regression, and **[curve fitting](#)**, allowing users to explore datasets and validate results with confidence. This makes Prism well suited for research, laboratory work, and academic analysis.

Prism also emphasizes the creation of clean, **[publication-quality graphs](#)** by offering flexible chart types and detailed formatting controls. These tools help turn complex datasets into clear visuals suitable for papers, presentations, and reports.

By combining reliable statistics with professional visual output, Prism is a trusted choice for scientists and analysts who need accurate results and clear communication on macOS.
